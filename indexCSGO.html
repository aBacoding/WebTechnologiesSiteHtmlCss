ABOUT CS:GO

MORE INFORMATION
Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a multiplayer computer game developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. The game was released for mobile computers on Windows and macOS operating systems,
as well as for the Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 game consoles on August 21, 2012.
In 2002, the site HLTV (HALF LIFE TV) was first created,
which today is a reliable resource for calculating the tops and ratings of players, also showing many tournaments
and other areas of the esports world in csgo

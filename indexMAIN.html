ABOUT Cybersport

Esports is a team or individual competition based on video games. It is recognized as an official sport in Russia. All esports disciplines are divided into several main classes, distinguished by the properties of spaces, models, a game task and the developed gaming skills of esports players: first-person shooters, real-time strategies, sports simulators, car simulators, flight simulators, fighting games, team role-playing games with elements of tactical and strategic games, etc.

Esport games

Dota 2

Dota 2 doesn't have the biggest gaming community, but it has the biggest prizes for the World Championship. The first world Dota 2 tournament was held in 2011. The champion of The International 2011 was the legendary composition of the Ukrainian NAVI team. The prize fund of this championship was $ 1.6 million.

Then and now the Dota 2 World Cup is being played for the biggest money in all esports, and the amount of prizes is growing at a furious pace. Thus, the prize fund of The International 2021 amounted to 39 million US dollars. The winning team, in 2021 it was Team Spirit, took home an impressive $18.2 million. The silver medalist, the PSG.LGD team, took $5.2 million. This amount is several times higher than the money earned by the winners of other esports games for a year, or even a couple of years.

As in the case of CS:GO, Dota 2 hosts a bunch of tournaments, leagues, majors, regional and sponsored championships. The largest among them are ESL One Birmingham, MDL Changsha Major, Epicenter XL, WESGG 2017, DAC and The Bucharest Major.

COUNTER-STRIKE: GLOBAL OFFENSIVE

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is one of the most popular esports games. Thanks to its long history and past games of the series, CS: GO attracts not only schoolchildren and students, but also gamers under 30 and older.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive hosts many tournaments, leagues and championships. The main one is considered to be World Electronic Sports Games. In 2017, its prize fund amounted to 1.5 million US dollars.

Besides him, the Intel Extreme Masters World Championship stands out from the major championships, where half a million dollars are played. Do not forget about the European esports league Electronic Sports League, Esl-League StarSeries, LEAGUE Major and dozens of small sponsored leagues.
